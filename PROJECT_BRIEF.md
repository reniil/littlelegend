# Project: LittleLegend 🌟
## (Formerly LifeCV)

---

## 🎯 Why "LittleLegend"?

**Problem with "LifeCV":**
- Sounds corporate/boring
- Kids don't connect with "CV"
- Feels like homework

**Why "LittleLegend":**
- Every child is a legend in the making ⭐
- Empowering and positive
- Works for ages 0-18
- Memorable and brandable
- Domain available (likely)
- Social media friendly

---

## 🌟 Alternative Names Considered

| Name | Pros | Cons |
|------|------|------|
| **LittleLegend** ⭐ | Empowering, memorable, ageless | - |
| Growfolio | Growth-focused | Sounds corporate |
| BloomBook | Natural metaphor | Too feminine? |
| StorySprout | Narrative focus | Hard to spell |
| StarPath | Aspirational | Generic |
| AchieveKids | Action-oriented | Boring |
| Milestone | Clear purpose | Overused |
| DreamBuilder | Inspiring | Long |

**Winner: LittleLegend** 🏆
